The Sungura Oath

By the rising sun and the open path,
I pledge myself to the spirit of Sungura.

I swear to seek adventure beyond the familiar,
to move with courage, mindfulness, curiosity, and quick spirit,
like the Sungura that never fears the unknown trail.

I promise loyalty to my fellow adventurers—
to stand together in challenge and discovery,
for no great journey is made alone.

I will respect the planet we explore,
protect the wild,
and leave every place better than we found it.

I will guard the secrets of the Sungura,
honor its name,
and carry its spirit wherever my journey leads.

From this day forward,
my path is failure,
my guide is courage,
and my tribe is Sungura.

So I swear.
